About us

Welcome to Cushy Coffee Co., your cozy spot for freshly brewed coffee and delicious pastries. We are a small coffee shop business located in the heart of downtown, serving our community with quality and care.

We started Cushy Coffee Co. in 2023 with a passion for coffee and a dream of creating a welcoming space for people to relax, connect, and enjoy. We source our beans from local roasters who share our commitment to sustainability and fair trade. We also bake our pastries daily using fresh ingredients and local suppliers.

Our mission is to provide you with the best coffee experience possible, whether you need a quick pick-me-up, a quiet place to work, or a friendly chat with our baristas. We love what we do, and we hope you do too.

Come and visit us today and see why we are more than just a coffee shop. We are Cushy Coffee Co., and we can't wait to meet you.
